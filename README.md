# Navi---Mental-Health-app

The idea is to have a fully integrated mental health companion app, that gives feedback and potential treatment options to a lisenced psychologist

Has a journaling feature that assesses depression score and sentimental score
  - AI (linear model) trained on Reddit Mental Health Dataset

With that score and other physiological info, a trained model would output treatment options that are most likely to fit your lifestyle and depression type
  - AI (random forest) trained on STAR*D dataset

Could begin to integrate other models for data incorporation and mood analysis
 - keyboard typing/stroke identifier
 - audio from voice (DAIC-WOZ dataset)
 - physiological data (HRV)
